Who We Are

At Cabled Wakeparks, we are passionate about revolutionizing the cable park industry. Born in Central Florida from a group of riders and industry professionals frustrated with the challenges facing the cable watersports industry in the United States, we are driven to create innovative solutions that make cable parks more efficient, sustainable, and profitable.

As a tech startup currently in the funding stage, we are seeking investors and technical collaborators to join us on this journey. Together, we can bring forward-thinking solutions to an industry ready for transformation.
